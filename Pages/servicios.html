Emissions Reports

At Interfuel we offer an optimized service for the entire reporting and compensation process as we have more than ten years in the field, having prepared and carried out monitoring plans and annual emissions reports for both large airlines and small operators.

No matter if it is a commercial airline or a private aviation company, we have got you covered as we were members of the team of technical advisers of the Global Market Measure Technical Task Force (GMTF) for the Committee on Aviation Environmental Protection (CAEP) for the analysis and development of the global emissions compensation scheme for the commercial aviation industry and we offer an optimized service to meet the new requirements implemented.
